Mitosis is the first EOL (Ecosystem-Owned Liquidity) protocol that redefines DeFi LP experience. Mitosis Expedition is the beginning of our journey to EOL and the base layer to bootstrap liquidity. The liquidity hosted by Expedition will be used for the Mitosis EOL program.

Besides opting into EOL, Mitosis Expedition offers a default layer of yields. The yield stack will thrive as Mitosis Expedition expands across more assets on diverse networks.

Join now to enjoy Omni-Sourced Yield. Learn more through the docs and the Mitosis Forum

Mitosis Vault is a smart contract built and deployed by Mitosis. Depositing to Mitosis means depositing your assets to the Mitosis Vault deployed on various networks that Mitosis Expedition supports. Your deposit position is represented by miAsset, which is issued to you when you make deposits to the Vault smart contracts.

The Mitosis Vault smart contracts were deployed after a thorough audit process with two firms specialized in smart contract audits.

1. Secure3 - Read the audit report here

2. Omniscia - Read the audit report here

In addition to previous audits, more audits with different audit firms are under process.

Yes, Mitosis EOL will utilize the underlying assets deposited to Mitosis Vaults.

Protocols that want to use the liquidity locked in Mitosis will post proposals (with a clear and exclusive reward scheme for Mitosis LPs) on the Mitosis Forum. After Forum discussions, there will be governance proposals that miAsset holders vote for. If the proposals pass in the community-driven EOL governance, protocols will have chances for getting the liquidity.

In other words, LPs’ assets in Mitosis Expedition will be further utilized in other protocols approved by the EOL governance, so LPs can enjoy the Omni-Sourced Yield.

Expedition participants earn MITO Points by depositing assets and holding miAssets. Accumulating MITO Points grants eligibility for Mitosis governance token airdrops.

As we support more assets, each asset will have its own Expedition, and MITO points will also be calculated separately for each type. MITO Point from each Expedition will have different allocations when it comes to future $MITO airdrops.

Mitosis Expedition offers various Boosts and Bonuses for the Campaign participants to earn more MITO Points. Learn more about the Boosts and Bonuses through the docs.

Redeem is only available when an Epoch is live and your desired network's deposit cap hasn't already been reached. Redeem is not available when there is no live Epoch and your desired network's deposit cap has already been reached.
